Applications of Differentiation

Q.1

Find the intervals in which the function
\(f(x) = \sin x +\cos x \space,\space 0\geq x\geq 2\pi \)
is strictly increasing or decreasing.

Q.2

The length x of a rectangle is decreasing at the rate of 5 cm/min and the width y is increasing at the rate of 4 cm/min. When x = 8 cm and y = 6 cm, find the rate of change of (i) the perimeter. (ii) area of rectangle.

Q.3

Find the points on the curve \(y = x^3\) at which the slope of the tangent is equal to y-coordinate of the point.

Q.4

Find the equations of the normal to the curve \(y= x^3 + 2x + 6 \), which are parallel to line \(x + 14y + 4 = 0\).

Q.5

Find the equations of tangents to the curve \(3x^2 - y^2 = 8 \), which passes through the point\( \left(\frac{4}{3},0\right) \).

Q.6

A tank with rectangular base and rectangular sides, open at the top is to be constructed, so that its depth is 2 m and volume is 8 \(m^3 \) If building of tank cost 70Rupees per sq m for the base and 45Rupees per sq m for sides. What is the cost of least expensive tank?
